From Good to Great: Pro-Tips

Small changes in phrasing can lead to dramatically better results. These pro-tips focus on clarity and positive framing to guide the model effectively.

Be Positive & Clear: Frame instructions affirmatively. Instead of "Don't use jargon," try "Explain this in simple terms for a beginner." This gives the AI a clear target.
Refine Iteratively: Treat prompting as a conversation. Use the first response as a starting point to add more detail and guide the AI closer to your goal.
